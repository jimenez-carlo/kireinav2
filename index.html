ABOUT KIREINA

Kireina Industry Co., Ltd.

Kireina Industry Co., Ltd. is one of the earliest manufacturers engaged in researching, developing, producing and selling smart film and smart glass. We commit ourselves to the field of global construction materials, home automation ,windows, doors and glass, automotive and supporting theses fields with high quality smart films and smart glass. Kireina films consist of imported high permeability liquid crystal film and high quality automotive float glass. Our products have been guaranteed quality management ISO9001 and environmental management system ISO14001.
