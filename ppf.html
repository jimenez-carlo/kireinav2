FAQ

How does Paint Protection Film self-heal?

The self-healing technology describes clear bra’s ability to return to its original form after washing marks, swirl-marks, or other light scratches are introduced into the surface of the film. Paint Protection Film will not heal if the film is fully punctured or torn.

How does Paint Protection Film self-heal?

No. A good clear bra is a virtually invisible urethane film that is both stain and fade resistant. It won’t impact the color of your car.

What do I need to do after application?

After application you should wait at least 7 days before washing the vehicle. After that, you can wash your vehicle as you normally would. If you are using a pressure washer, be sure to stay 8–12 inches from the edge of the film in order to prevent peeling. If using compressed air, keep the nozzle one foot away from exposed edges. Avoid wiping or cleaning the film in direct sunlight. If the film is not covered with a Ceramic Coating, you should apply a high-quality wax or sealant regularly. Finally, do not use a powered buffer on the film.
